# Prior Incident: North Vault Excursion

During a March drill, Zone A rose above the release threshold while an outbound shipment was already staged.

- Backup cooling restored trend stability within 7 minutes.
- Batch VX-188 was quarantined before QA disposition.
- Shipment SHP-602 was held at dock until the chamber was verified stable.
- The highest-confidence evidence came from the SOP, dashboard screenshot, and incident log together.

Lesson learned: hold the shipment before warehouse handoff, and quarantine the affected batch even if the excursion looks brief.
